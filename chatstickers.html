ChatStickers

Chat stickers i designed for my school project using Adobe Illustrator.

The first slide showcases the final product, while the other two slides feature concept sketches that contributed to the creation of the final design

Design Rationale:

The chat stickers are charming and playful, featuring cute characters and a soft color palette. Each sticker, like the Panda with Fluff, which suggests exhaustion, or the Panda with Glasses and "Hi" for friendly greetings, effectively conveys different emotions. The stickers, including the humorous Boy with Burger and the Sad Boy with Bun Head, are simple and clear, with a consistent style and relatable expressions. They are well-designed for adding personality to casual online conversations and are likely to appeal to users who enjoy cute and relatable characters.
